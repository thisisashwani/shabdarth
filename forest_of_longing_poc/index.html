Bekhudi

"Jisey hosh ki duniya bhaaye, woh bekhudi kya jaane?"

Bekhudi

A blissful surrender; a state of forgetful ecstasy.

Origin: Persian — 'bē' (without) + 'khudī' (self)

Song Line: Hosh walon ko khabar kya, bekhudi kya cheez hai

💌 A love letter floating in the wind

Fizaayein

"Kabhi socha hai… hawa sirf chalti nahi, yaadein bhi le jaati hai?"

Fizaayein

Expansive winds, symbolic of changing atmospheres or emotions

Origin: Arabic 'faḍāʼ' meaning 'open space'

Song Line: Fizaayein bhi hoon main, hawaon jaisa

Zulfon

May-kashi

"Yeh nashe ki baat nahi… yeh toh rooh ki pyaas hai."

May-kashi

The act of drinking wine; metaphor for deep emotional surrender

Origin: Persian — 'may' (wine) + 'kashi' (drawing out)

Song Line: May-kashi mein dooba tha uski baaton ka nasha

🍷 A fragment of Rumi’s poetry about spiritual intoxication
